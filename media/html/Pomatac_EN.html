MULTIVARIATE ORTHOGONAL POLYNOMIALS: THEORETICAL ASPECTS AND SCIENTIFIC APPLICATIONS

  • Funded by the Ministry of Sciences, Innovation and Universities (MICINN) of Spain and the European Regional Development Fund (ERDF).
    Call of 2018: Projects I+D for Generation of Knowledge, of the National Program for Development of Scientific Research and Technical Excellence of the I+D+i system.

Duration

  • From 01/01/2019 until 12/31/2022.

Abstract

The general objective of the project is the improvement on the knowledge of the theory of Multivariate Orthogonal Polynomials, which are per se an interesting subject of research and, simultaneously show relevance in their scientific and technological applications.
Multivariate orthogonal polynomials are not only a simple generalization of the polynomials in a single variable but they are indeed very complex mathematical objects with singular properties.
This Research Project can be seen as a extended continuation of the Research Projects: MTM2014-53171-P, MTM2011-28952-C02-02, MTM2008-06689-C02-02).
